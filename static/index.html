About

From Chick-fil-A to Software Development

England Systems grew out of my work in finance across two Chick-fil-A locations in Tulsa, Oklahoma. I taught myself software development by solving real operational problems: invoices, translation workflows, birthday-message automation, websites, reporting graphs, and tools that made work clearer.

The work starts by understanding how the business actually runs, then removing friction with practical systems, useful software, better documentation, or team education.
