About England Systems

Built from firsthand small-business operations.

I'm Phillip England. England Systems grew out of my finance and operations work across two Chick-fil-A locations in Tulsa, where I learned software development by solving problems the business dealt with every day.

That work included invoice systems, translation workflows, automated team communication, websites, reporting graphs, and internal tools. It shaped how I approach every project now: understand the work first, build the simplest useful solution, and make sure the people using it can rely on it.
